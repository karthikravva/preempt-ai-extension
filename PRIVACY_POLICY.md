# Privacy Policy for Preempt AI Browser Extension

**Last Updated: December 9, 2024**

## Overview

Preempt AI ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our browser extension.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Prompt Text**: When you use the extension to scan a prompt, the text is sent to our API for security analysis.
- **No Personal Information**: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information about you.

### What We Do NOT Collect
- Browsing history
- Cookies or tracking data
- Personal identification information
- Login credentials
- Any data from websites you visit (other than prompts you explicitly scan)

## Data Processing

### How Your Data Is Processed
1. When you click "Scan" or use the Preempt protection feature, your prompt text is sent to our secure API.
2. The API analyzes the text for security threats and sensitive data (PII).
3. A sanitized version is returned to your browser.
4. **No prompts are stored or logged on our servers.**

### Data Retention
- We do not retain or store any prompt data after processing.
- Each request is processed in real-time and immediately discarded.

## Third-Party Services

The extension communicates with:
- **Preempt API** (`preempt-production.up.railway.app`): For security analysis and PII detection.

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

## Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following permissions:

- **activeTab**: To access the current tab when you activate the extension.
- **storage**: To save your preferences (like protection toggle state).
- **contextMenus**: To provide right-click menu options.
- **host_permissions**: To inject the protection features on AI chat websites.

## Security

- All communication with our API uses HTTPS encryption.
- We follow industry-standard security practices.
- No sensitive data is stored locally or remotely.

## Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
